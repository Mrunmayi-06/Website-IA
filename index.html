About InAmigos Foundation

A movement born from
love for India

InAmigos Foundation is a registered nonprofit working across six critical domains — food security, education, environment, animal welfare, rural development, and women empowerment. We are 100% volunteer-powered.

🎯
Our Mission

To build a self-reliant, equitable, and compassionate India — one community at a time.

👁️
Our Vision

An India where no child goes hungry, no forest is lost, and every person has dignity.

💯
100% Transparent

All legal documents are accessible via the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. We maintain open financial records.
